AIWire Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AIWire ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the AIWire mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google we receive your name and email address as provided by those identity providers.

Usage data: We collect information about how you use the app, including articles you read, bookmarks you save, and search queries you submit. This data is used to personalise your experience.

Content sent to AI: When you use AI-powered features, certain content is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. This includes:

  • Article text — when you request an AI summary.
  • Resume content — when you upload a resume for career analysis, ATS scoring, and job matching.
  • Mock interview answers — your spoken or typed responses are sent for AI scoring.
  • Career profile data — your skills and experience level for personalised recommendations.
  • Precise location — when you use the "Near Me" jobs feature (used only to find nearby jobs, not stored).
  • Voice/microphone input — when you speak interview answers (processed locally by speech recognition, then text is sent to AI).

We do not store resume content, interview answers, or voice data on our servers. Anthropic does not use your data to train their models via the API.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To personalise your news feed and AI-generated summaries.
  • To process subscription payments via Apple StoreKit / Google Play Billing.
  • To analyse aggregate usage trends and improve app features.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude API) — Powers all AI features. Content is sent to Anthropic servers to generate responses. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train its models without consent.

Apple Sign In / Google Sign In — Authentication only. Governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

NewsAPI — Supplies news headlines. No personal data is shared.

Apple StoreKit / Google Play Billing — Manages subscriptions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google; we receive subscription status only.

Firebase Analytics — Anonymised usage analytics. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

OpenStreetMap Nominatim — Reverse geocodes your GPS coordinates to a city name for job search. No personal data is stored by this service.

4. Data Retention

Account and usage data is retained as long as your account is active. All data is stored on-device using your device's secure storage. If you delete the app, all local data is removed.

5. Children's Privacy

AIWire is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at email2prab@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at email2prab@gmail.com
